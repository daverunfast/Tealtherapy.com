Services

Pediatric occupational therapy services assist a child in engaging in their occupations or daily activities. Childhood occupations include play, self care, learning, and socializing.
Pediatric occupational therapy addresses: Sensory processing, Fine motor skills and coordination, Gross motor skills and coordination, Visual perception , Visual motor skills, Handwriting, Motor planning, Bilateral motor integration, Sensory processing, Self care and Social skills.

About

Kelly Barrett, M.S., OTR/L

Owner and Pediatric Occupational Therapist

Kelly graduated from Winston-Salem State University in 2008 with a Master of Science in Occupational Therapy. She has been practicing pediatric occupational therapy in an outpatient sensory integration clinic setting since January 2009. Kelly has received continuing education and training in the areas of sensory integration and sensory processing, formulation of sensory diets, autism spectrum disorders, neurodevelopmental treatment, Handwriting Without Tears®, Attention Deficit and Hyperactivity Disorder, visual processing, feeding disorders, and nutrition.
